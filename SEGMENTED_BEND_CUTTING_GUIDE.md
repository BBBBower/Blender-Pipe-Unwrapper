## TECHNICAL CALCULATIONS

### Why Different Widths for Each Layer?

The stainless layer is slightly wider (0.9mm more) because it wraps at a larger radius over the fiberglass:

**Fiberglass Layer:**
- Wraps at diameter: 76.2 + (2 × 6.0) = 88.2mm
- Circumference: π × 88.2 = 277.1mm
- With 25mm overlap = 302.1mm

**Stainless Layer:**
- Wraps at diameter: 88.2 + (2 × 0.15) = 88.5mm
- Circumference: π × 88.5 = 278.0mm
- With 25mm overlap = 303.0mm

### Arc Length Calculation:

Each segment covers 18° (π/10 radians) of the bend:

**Fiberglass:**
- Layer radius: 114.3 + 6.0 = 120.3mm
- Arc length: 120.3 × (π/10) = 37.79mm

**Stainless:**
- Layer radius: 114.3 + 6.0 + 0.15 = 120.45mm
- Arc length: 120.45 × (π/10) = 37.84mm

- ✓ Check you have enough material for 5 pieces of each layer

### After Cutting:
- ✓ Measure each piece to ensure accuracy
- ✓ Width should be 302.1mm ± 1mm (fiberglass) or 303.0mm ± 1mm (stainless)
- ✓ Length should be 37.8mm ± 0.5mm for both

### During Wrapping:
- ✓ Ensure 25mm overlap is consistent
- ✓ Fiberglass sits directly on pipe (no gaps)
- ✓ Stainless sits directly on fiberglass (no gaps)
- ✓ Edges align properly when wrapping

## OPTIMIZATION TIPS

1. **Cutting Layout:**
   - All 5 pieces are identical
   - Can nest cuts efficiently on sheet material
   - Arrange pieces to minimize waste

2. **Batch Processing:**
   - Cut all 5 fiberglass pieces at once
   - Cut all 5 stainless pieces at once
   - Label each piece (1-5) to track during assembly

3. **Material Ordering:**
   - Fiberglass sheet: Need at least 302 × 189mm (or 5 × 38mm length)
   - Stainless sheet: Need at least 303 × 189mm (or 5 × 38mm length)
   - Add 10% for cutting errors and waste

## TROUBLESHOOTING

**Problem:** Template doesn't wrap fully around pipe
- **Solution:** Check pipe OD measurement - must be exactly 76.2mm

**Problem:** Gap between segments when assembled
- **Solution:** Ensure each segment is cut to exact 37.8mm length

**Problem:** Overlap is too tight or too loose
- **Solution:** Verify 25mm overlap is measured correctly from edge

**Problem:** Stainless doesn't fit over fiberglass
- **Solution:** Confirm fiberglass is exactly 6.0mm thick
